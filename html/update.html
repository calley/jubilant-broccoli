welcome to pandora enhancer


Thank you for downloading!

v4.1 is the latest version for Pandora Enhancer.

features

Notifications

After allowing notifications (simply visit pandora.com and it will walk you through this process) you will recieve desktop notifications of the current song playing. Notifications will respond differently when clicked and this can be changed in the extension Preferences

Preferences

Customize the extension with a few different settings: how long the notification will last and how to control the current song by clicking the notification. Simply click the extension icon to access this menu.

Ad Block

Get rid of those annoying ads so you can keep enjoying your music! (Note- video ads may still begin to load, but they will not play)

"Are You Still Listening?"

This pop-up would appear after a little time of inactivity. Pandora Enhancer gets rid of this so you can keep enjoying your music- nonstop

Downloads Button

Download the songs you've listened to as an .mp4 file! (The button is underneath the song information)

View Past Songs

View past songs you've listened to and play them again!

Pandora Controls

Newest addition to Pandora Enhancer- the ability to play/pause/skip or thumb up/down the current track being played!
